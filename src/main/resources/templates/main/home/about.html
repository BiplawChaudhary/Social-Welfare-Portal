Social Welfare Portal represents a government initiative dedicated to advancing social welfare in Nepal. We are a passionate team committed to transparency, inclusivity, and collaboration, serving as a bridge between the government and the people. Our values drive our actions as we strive to create a more inclusive and connected Nepal for everyone.
At Social Welfare Portal, our mission is clear and unwavering: to connect individuals, organizations, and communities in Nepal and empower them to create a more equitable and compassionate society. We believe that by working together, we can make a meaningful and lasting impact on the lives of our fellow citizens.
Our Mission
Our platform serves as a catalyst for change in Nepal, facilitating partnerships and collaborations between individuals, nonprofits, and businesses. We believe in the power of raising awareness about critical issues and supporting those actively driving positive change. Through grants, mentorship, and networking, we empower change makers and amplify their efforts for the betterment of Nepal.
