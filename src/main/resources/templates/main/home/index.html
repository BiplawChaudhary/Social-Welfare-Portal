Reducing Hunger
At Social Welfare Council, our unwavering mission is to make a positive impact on rural communities by ensuring access to nutritious food. With deep-rooted compassion and a commitment to addressing food insecurity, we tirelessly strive to provide sustenance to those in need. Through dedicated efforts and community partnerships, we extend a helping hand to remote and underserved areas, ensuring that no one goes to bed hungry.
Disaster Management
Social Welfare Council is dedicated to rehabilitation efforts in response to the devastating impact of flooding, one of nature's most destructive forces, causing erosion of agricultural lands and displacement of communities. We have established numerous warehouses across over 40 districts, strategically positioned to collect a wide range of emergency supplies and necessities.
